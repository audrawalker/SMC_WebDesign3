Executive Board Members

Silver Pelegrine is managed by a three-member Executive Board. Peter Gerber is Chairman of the Executive Board and Chief Executive Officer of Lufthansa Cargo. As of April 1, 2020, Peter Gerber took over as Chief Financal Officer and Labour Director an interim basis. Dorothea von Boxberg is Member of the Executive Board and Chief Commercial Officer, Harald Gloy is Member of the Executive Board and Chief Operating Officer.

Chairman

Peter Gerber has been the Chairman of the Executive Board and CEO of since May 2014. As of April 1, 2020, Gerber took over the Finance and Human Resources department on an interim basis. Mr. Gerber also assumes the role of Labor Director.Besides his duties as CEO, Gerber serves as Supervisory Board member of Albatros and Fraport AG and on the Executive Board of BVL, the German supply chain network, and German airline industry associations BDF and BDL. In September 2017, Gerber took over as Chairman of the IATA Cargo Advisory Council.

Operations

As Member of the Silver Pelegrine Incorportations Executive Board and Chief Operation Officer since January 1, 2019, Harald Gloy is responsible for Operations which comprises Handling Frankfurt, Handling Munich, Global Handling Management, Flight Operations & Transport Management, Security Management and Managing Customer Service.
