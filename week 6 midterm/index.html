Who We Are

Established in 1985 as the air freight division of Silver Pelegrine, we are the largest international cargo airline in the world. We connect the world's supply chains through 300+ destinations across 80 countries in 6 continents through Silver Pelegrine SkyCentral, our operations hub at Dubai International Airport (DXB) and Dubai World Central (DWC).
