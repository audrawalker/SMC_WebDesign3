Who we are and what we believe in

We founded Highly Likely in 2010 as a family: Anya & Yasha Michelson, a husband-and-wife design team, along with our daughter, Marina, a filmmaker and actor. Over the last 10 years, we have proudly extended our family to include a fantastic team of baristas, servers, cooks, and dishwashers, and a community of regular customers.

Events we like to party

What can we say — we love to host! Pop-up trucks, guest chefs, Sunday Suppers, and more. Check out the our events schedule to ensure you don’t get FOMO anytime soon.
